## 6. Closed gap — diff summary

`artifacts/api-server/e2e/run.ts` → `registerUploadsWithApi` now:

1. Maps the placeholder manifest's human-readable `consumed_by` label
   (`"Pre-Design"`, `"Lead Intake"`, `"Construction"`, `"Permits"`, …) onto
   the `POST /api/projects/:id/documents` `category` enum
   (`design`, `client_review`, `construction`, `permits`, fallback `otros`).
2. Adds `photoCategory` (`final` for signature images,
   `construction_progress` for construction photos,
   `punchlist_evidence` for permit-stage photos,
   `site_conditions` otherwise) for every image upload, satisfying the
   route's `photoCategory required for photo uploads` 400 contract.

No fixture, no schema, no route changes. The 22-item matrix and the four-gate
model are unchanged.
